Treatment for ear infection

Description

Status after 21 days treatment of the ear infection acute otitis externa (Van Balen et al., 2003).

Van Balen et al. (2003) report a randomized, double-blind, controlled trial comparing three treatments for an ear infection. The numbers and proportions of patients reported cured and not cured after 21 days of treatment are summarized in Table 7.3. Because there is no ordering between the treatments, we regard Table 7.3 as an unordered 3 Ã— 2 table.
