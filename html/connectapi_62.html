get_jobs R Documentation

Get Jobs

Description

Retrieve details about jobs associated with a content_item. "Jobs" in Posit Connect are content executions

Usage

get_jobs(content)

get_job(content, key)

Arguments

content

A Content object, as returned by content_item()
key

The key for a job
