nhanes_example R Documentation

Example Data from National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey ('NHANES')

Description

Example Data from National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey ('NHANES')

Usage

nhanes_example

Format

A data.frame with 7 columns, which are:

SEQN

ID of participant

mortstat

mortality status, 1-died, 0 - censored

event_time_years

time observed

WTMEC4YR_norm

weights normalized for survey

gender

gender

age_years_interview

age in years at interview

education_adult

educational status
