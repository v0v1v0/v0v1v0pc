Description

Retrieves the Continuous Ranked Probability Score (CRPS) [1].

Usage

getCRPS(z.pred, mean.pred, sd.pred)

Arguments

z.pred

(numeric vector).
mean.pred

(numeric vector).
sd.pred

(numeric vector).

Value

(numeric vector) retrieves CRPS.

Author(s)

Federico Blasi

References
