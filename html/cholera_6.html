Usage

Numeric. Size of point.

Character. Color of point.
pch

Numeric. Type of of point.
add.label

Logical. Add text annotation: "40 Broad Street"
text.size

Numeric. Size of text label.

Add base R point and (optionally) text to a graphics plot.
