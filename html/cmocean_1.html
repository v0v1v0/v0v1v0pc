cmocean-package R Documentation

Beautiful Colour Maps for Oceanography

Description

Perceptually uniform palettes for commonly used variables in oceanography as functions taking an integer and producing character vectors of colours. See Thyng, K.M., Greene, C.A., Hetland, R.D., Zimmerle, H.M. and S.F. DiMarco (2016) <doi:10.5670/oceanog.2016.66> for the guidelines adhered to when creating the palettes.

References

Thyng, K. M., Greene, C. A., Hetland, R. D., Zimmerle, H. M., and DiMarco, S. F. (2016). True colors of oceanography. Oceanography, 29(3), 10.

See Also

cmocean for the classic-style pallette-generating function, scale_colour_cmocean for ggplot2 helpers.
