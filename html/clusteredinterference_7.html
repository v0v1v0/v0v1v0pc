toy_data R Documentation

A "toy" dataset for illustrating the estimator

Description

A dataset containing simulated data for outcome, treatment status, cluster membership, and two pretreatment covariates. There are 22 clusters that have 3 individuals each, and 8 clusters that have 4 individuals each.

Usage

toy_data

Format

A data frame with 98 rows and 5 variables:

Outcome

Individual outcome status observed at the end of follow-up. E.g., infection status

Treatment

Individual treatment status observed at the end of follow-up. E.g., vaccination status

Cluster_ID

Unique identifier for the cluster of which the individual is a member

Age

Individual's age, in years

Distance

Individual's distance to river, in kilometers
